Too, to, two - Brexit

A lesson for today, “Too, to and two”.

Fill in the gaps:

Boris Johnson is offering parliament more time _ pass his Brexit deal in return for an election on December 12. The prime minister will tonight start his third attempt _ send voters _ the polls by tabling a motion under the Fixed-term Parliaments Act. MPs will vote on Monday on whether the UK will have its first December election since 1923. _ thirds of MPs need _ vote for the poll. Is it _ late to hold an election before Brexit?

  1. Two, too, to, to, to, to
  2. to, to, to, Two, too, to
  3. to, to, to, Two, to, too
  4. too, to, two, to, to, to

Post your answer as a comment on the home page.

The correct answer for our recent test “Too, to and two. Fill in the gaps: Brexit” is 3. Good work everyone.
